Meet Henry

A Natick dad with
a really long punch list.

For years, Henry Pollock spent his weekends doing what most dads do — fixing, building, and improving the family home in Natick, MA. He had enthusiastic little helpers along the way, and together they tackled everything from wobbly railings to full room renovations.

Now that the kids are grown and the house is finally done, Henry has a new mission: helping other families stay on top of their own punch lists.

Whether you're a busy parent, a new homeowner, or just someone who'd rather spend the weekend doing literally anything else — Henry brings the same care and attention to your home that he gave to his own.
