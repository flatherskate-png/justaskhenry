Meet Henry

A Natick neighbor who
knows his way around a home.

Henry Pollock grew up in Northern Ireland and has spent most of his adult life figuring out how things work — first as an engineer, then as a homeowner, then as a landlord juggling the endless demands of rental properties. Along the way, he accumulated a lot of skills, a lot of knowledge, and frankly, a lot of tools.

Carpentry is his favorite. But there's not much he hasn't tackled — floors, walls, patios, decks, fixtures, repairs of every description. He's the kind of person who actually enjoys the problem-solving part, which means he'll think carefully about your situation before picking up a single tool.

He'll also tell you honestly if something isn't worth doing, or if you need someone else. That's just how he operates.

Henry lives in Natick with his family and serves the surrounding MetroWest communities.
